Mock-ups

Mock-ups are an essential tool for visualizing ideas and bringing concepts to life. For customers, they offer a clear and tangible way to see how a design will look and function in the real world before committing to the final product. Whether it’s a website, logo, packaging, or any other design, mock-ups help bridge the gap between imagination and reality. By using mock-ups, you can evaluate the design’s look, feel, and practicality in context. It allows you to spot potential improvements, refine details, and ensure the result aligns with your vision. Mock-ups also make collaboration more effective, giving you and the designer a shared understanding of the project’s direction. At Jelle Design, we create realistic and engaging mock-ups to give you confidence in the process and the final outcome. They’re not just previews—they’re a vital step in delivering designs that exceed your expectations.

Bootstrap Grid

The Bootstrap Grid system is an invaluable tool for developers, providing a responsive and flexible framework to create layouts that work seamlessly across all devices. By using a 12-column grid system, Bootstrap makes it easy to align, organize, and structure content, ensuring a consistent design regardless of screen size. For developers, the grid system simplifies the process of creating responsive websites by eliminating the need to write complex CSS for layout adjustments. With predefined classes for columns, rows, and containers, you can quickly build layouts that automatically adapt to different resolutions and orientations. The grid also allows for nesting, offsetting, and reordering columns, giving developers precise control over design structure without extra coding effort. Bootstrap’s grid promotes efficiency, consistency, and scalability, making it a go-to solution for both beginners and seasoned developers. Whether you’re prototyping or building a full-scale application, the Bootstrap Grid empowers you to focus on creativity while ensuring robust and professional results.
